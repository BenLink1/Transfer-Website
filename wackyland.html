Nebuchadnezzer is a character from the Bible who was also, I think, an historical king of Babylon, or maybe it was Sumeria. Anyhow, this is a painting by William Blake depicting the phase of his life in which he'd become insane and bestial. It's noteable because it's the most unnerving facial expression I've ever seen with the possibly exception of a shot of Javier Bardem in the film No Country For Old Men. You know the one I mean. Where he's smiling as he says "step out of the car" before he murders a driver using a cattle stunner.
